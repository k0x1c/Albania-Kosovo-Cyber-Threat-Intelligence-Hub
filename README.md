# Albania–Kosovo Cyber Threat Intelligence Hub

Welcome to the official GitHub repository of the **Albania–Kosovo Cyber Threat Intelligence Hub**.

This repository serves as a central place for sharing resources, research, tools, and documentation related to cyber threat intelligence and cybersecurity. It supports the hub’s mission to strengthen regional cyber resilience and contribute to the broader cybersecurity and intelligence community.

## Purpose

The Albania–Kosovo Cyber Threat Intelligence Hub was created to unite cybersecurity and intelligence professionals, experts, and enthusiasts from Albania and Kosovo. Our goal is to encourage collaboration, knowledge exchange, and practical contributions in the field of cyber threat intelligence.

## What You’ll Find Here

This repository may include:
- Threat intelligence research and analysis
- Documentation and educational materials
- Community-driven tools and scripts
- Blog content and technical write-ups
- Guidelines, best practices, and reference material

Content will evolve as the hub grows and new initiatives are introduced.

## Who This Repository Is For

- Cyber threat intelligence analysts  
- Cybersecurity professionals and researchers  
- Intelligence practitioners  
- Students and enthusiasts with an interest in CTI and cybersecurity
